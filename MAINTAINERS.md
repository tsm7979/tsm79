## Maintainer responsibilities

- Triage incoming issues within 5 business days
- Triage incoming PRs within 5 business days
- Acknowledge security reports within 48 hours (see [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md))
- Cut releases on the published schedule
- Keep the public roadmap honest (see [ROADMAP.md](ROADMAP.md))
- Enforce the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md)

## Becoming a maintainer

Contributors are considered for maintainer status after they have:

1. Landed at least **10 substantive PRs** to `main`
2. Reviewed at least **20 PRs** from others with the same thoroughness as a maintainer
3. Demonstrated judgement on at least one architectural decision (an RFC, a deprecation, a security-sensitive change)
4. Subscribed to the [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) and the [Security Policy](SECURITY.md)

Existing maintainers propose new maintainers in a private thread and decide by lazy consensus over a 5-day window.

## Stepping down

Maintainers may step down at any time. They retain `emeritus` recognition in this file. They are not expected to keep responding to pings — please address questions to active maintainers.
