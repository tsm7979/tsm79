The exposure

Every day your apps send millions of prompts to models you don't own — carrying secrets, customer PII, and source code straight past your perimeter. One leaked key. One unredacted record. One prompt you can't prove you logged.

LLMs are in production everywhere. Almost no one has inline control over what those prompts contain — until they leak.
How it works

Every prompt goes through TSM first — it scans for PII & secrets, redacts them, routes sensitive calls to a local model, blocks what must never leave, and logs everything to a tamper-evident ledger.
